2018 Annual Meeting 2018年年会

(May 5, 2018) Buffett is “delighted” Apple is buying back $100B of its shares as Berkshire dramatically increases its stake in the iPhone maker. He also reveals why Berkshire won’t be buying Microsoft stock, predicts cryptocurrencies will come to “bad endings,” describes the goals for his new partnership with Amazon and JPMorgan on health care costs, and gives a master class on ignoring news headlines while making investment decisions.  
（2018 年 5 月 5 日）巴菲特对苹果公司回购价值 1000 亿美元的股票表示“欣喜”，伯克希尔哈撒韦公司也因此大幅增持了这家 iPhone 制造商的股份。他还透露了伯克希尔哈撒韦公司为何不会购买微软股票，预测加密货币最终会走向“糟糕的结局”，阐述了他与亚马逊和摩根大通在医疗保健成本方面新合作的目标，并就如何在投资决策中忽略新闻标题进行了深入讲解。

Berkshire Hathaway Shareholder Letters —  
伯克希尔哈撒韦公司致股东信——

In 2017, Berkshire’s 23.0 percent increase in per-share book value and a 21.9 percent gain for its stock both slightly outpaced the S&P’s 21.8 percent advance. Berkshire’s net worth soared by $65.3 billion during the year. But in his annual letter, Buffett noted that $29 billion of that was “delivered” by Congressional passage of a big tax-cut bill.  
2017年，伯克希尔哈撒韦公司每股账面价值增长23.0%，股价上涨21.9%，均略高于标普500指数21.8%的涨幅。伯克希尔哈撒韦公司的净资产在这一年飙升了653亿美元。但巴菲特在年度致股东信中指出，其中290亿美元是由于国会通过了一项大规模减税法案而“获得的”。
