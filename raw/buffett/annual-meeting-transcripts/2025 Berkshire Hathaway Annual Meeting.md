2025 Annual Meeting 2025年年会

(May 3, 2025) As the meeting is ending, Warren Buffett stuns shareholders by announcing he is ready to step down as Berkshire Hathaway’s CEO at the end of the year. He will ask the board to give the job to Vice Chair Greg Abel, the executive who was designated years to be Buffett’s successor as chief executive officer. Earlier, Buffett, Abel and insurance chief Ajit Jain discuss tariffs and trade, Berkshire’s enormous cash pile, and why investors shouldn’t overreact to the stock market’s wild swings in the first few months of President Trump’s second term.  
（2025年5月3日）会议即将结束时，沃伦·巴菲特突然宣布他将于年底卸任伯克希尔·哈撒韦公司首席执行官一职，令股东们震惊不已。他将请求董事会把这个职位交给副董事长格雷格·阿贝尔，阿贝尔多年来一直被认为是巴菲特的接班人。此前，巴菲特、阿贝尔和保险业总裁阿吉特·贾恩讨论了关税和贸易问题、伯克希尔庞大的现金储备，以及投资者为何不应在特朗普总统第二个任期开始的头几个月对股市的剧烈波动反应过度。

Berkshire Hathaway Shareholder Letters —  
伯克希尔哈撒韦公司致股东信——

Berkshire Hathaway’s per-share market value increased by 25.5% in 2024, giving it a nine-year winning streak. The benchmark S&P 500 index with dividends included almost matched Berkshire’s performance with a gain of 25.0% for its second straight annual increase. In his letter, Buffett did not explain why the company’s cash had reached a record $334 billion. He did downplay its size, writing, “Despite what some commentators currently view as an extraordinary cash position at Berkshire, the great majority of your money remains in equities,” in the form of both publicly-traded stocks and wholly-owned subsidiaries. He also noted that since is 94 years old, it would not be long before Greg Abel replaces him as CEO. He did not, however, provide any specifics on when that might happen.  
伯克希尔·哈撒韦公司2024年的每股市值增长了25.5%，实现了连续第九年的增长。包含股息的标普500指数表现几乎与伯克希尔持平，上涨了25.0%，连续第二年实现年度增长。巴菲特在信中并未解释公司现金为何达到创纪录的3340亿美元。他淡化了这一规模，写道：“尽管一些评论员目前认为伯克希尔的现金储备非同寻常，但你们的大部分资金仍然投资于股票”，包括上市股票和全资子公司。他还指出，由于他已94岁高龄，格雷格·阿贝尔很快就会接替他担任首席执行官。但他并未透露具体时间。
