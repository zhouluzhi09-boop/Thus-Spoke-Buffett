2023 Annual Meeting 2023年年会

(May 6, 2023) As attendance returned to normal levels at the second in-person shareholders meeting since the COVID pandemic started, Warren Buffett revealed he has no intention of bidding for full control of Occidental Petroleum despite building a stake in the oil giant, called on the U.S. government to explicitly guarantee deposits of all sizes in the wake of recent bank failures, and called Apple a “better business” than any company completely owned by Berkshire. Charlie Munger criticized both the U.S. and China for “stupid, stupid, stupid” economic tensions that are preventing both countries from what could be mutually-beneficial trade relations.  
（2023年5月6日）自新冠疫情爆发以来，伯克希尔·哈撒韦公司第二次举行线下股东大会，出席人数恢复正常水平。会上，沃伦·巴菲特透露，尽管他已增持西方石油公司的股份，但他无意竞购该公司的全部控制权。他还呼吁美国政府在近期银行倒闭潮后，明确为所有规模的存款提供担保，并称苹果公司比伯克希尔·哈撒韦公司全资拥有的任何一家公司都“更胜一筹”。查理·芒格则批评美中两国之间“愚蠢至极”的经济紧张关系，认为这种关系阻碍了两国建立互利共赢的贸易关系。

Berkshire Hathaway Shareholder Letters —  
伯克希尔哈撒韦公司致股东信——

Berkshire Hathaway’s per-share market value increased by 4.0% in 2022, easily outperforming the S&P 500′s 18.1% plunge as the Federal Reserve aggressively raised interest rates to tame an inflation surge. In his letter to shareholders, Buffett didn’t offer any commentary on the Fed’s hikes or the threat they could put the economy into a recession. He did, however, sharply criticize Washington politicians, mostly Democrats, who had been attacking corporate buybacks as “self-indulgence” that hurts their workers and the economy. “When you are told that all repurchases are harmful to shareholders or to the country, or particularly beneficial to CEOs, you are listening to either an economic illiterate or a silver-tongued demagogue (characters that are not mutually exclusive).”  
2022年，伯克希尔·哈撒韦公司的每股市值增长了4.0%，轻松跑赢标普500指数18.1%的暴跌。当时，美联储为了抑制通胀飙升而大幅加息。在致股东的信中，巴菲特并未对美联储的加息及其可能导致经济衰退的威胁发表任何评论。然而，他严厉批评了华盛顿的政客，尤其是民主党人，他们一直抨击公司回购股票是“自我放纵”，损害了员工和经济。“当你听到有人说所有回购都对股东或国家有害，或者对首席执行官特别有利时，你听的要么是一个经济文盲，要么是一个巧舌如簧的煽动者（这两种角色并非互斥）。”
