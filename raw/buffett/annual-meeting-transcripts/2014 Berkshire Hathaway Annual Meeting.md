2014 Annual Meeting 2014年年会

(May 3, 2014) Buffett explains why he, as a member of Coca-Cola’s board, abstained on a vote approving an executive compensation plan he thought was “excessive.” He also argues that CEO’s salaries should be kept secret, criticizes some activist investors for their short-term goals, and rejects the view that corporate taxes are too high.  
（2014年5月3日）巴菲特解释了他作为可口可乐董事会成员，为何在批准一项他认为“过高”的高管薪酬方案的投票中投了弃权票。他还认为首席执行官的薪酬应该保密，批评一些激进投资者的短期目标，并驳斥了企业税过高的观点。

Berkshire Hathaway Shareholder Letters —  
伯克希尔哈撒韦公司致股东信——

For a second year, Berkshire’s per-share book value underperformed the S&P 500, but not by much. It was up 10.5 percent vs. the benchmark index’s 10.9 percent gain. In his annual letter, Buffett wrote he ”“struck out”″ on several potential multi-billion dollar acquisitions, on top of finding only a few attractive stocks to buy. As a result, Berkshire ended the year with $43 billion in cash, ”“not a happy position.”  
伯克希尔哈撒韦公司的每股账面价值连续第二年跑输标普500指数，但差距不大。其涨幅为10.5%，而基准指数的涨幅为10.9%。巴菲特在年度致股东信中写道，他错失了几个潜在的数十亿美元收购机会，而且只找到了少数几只值得买入的股票。因此，伯克希尔哈撒韦公司年底持有430亿美元的现金，“这并非一个令人满意的局面”。
